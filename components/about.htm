Who We Are

Established at the College of Engineering Vadakara (CEV), the FOSS Club is a student-driven initiative dedicated to nurturing skilled programmers and developers. We believe that technology should be open, accessible, and collaborative.

Our primary goal is to provide a hands-on environment where students can master FOSS technologies, fostering a culture of innovation that is essential for building a strong foundation in software development.

Our Mission

To demystify technology and provide students with the resources to contribute to open-source projects that impact the real world.

Our Community

Supported by FOSS United, we are a network of mentors, learners, and creators working together to build a better digital future.
