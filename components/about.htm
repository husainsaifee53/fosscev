About FOSS Club CEV

The FOSS Club at College of Engineering, Vadakara has been established with a visionary goal: to nurture the next generation of skilled programmers and developers who will shape the future of technology. We believe in the power of Free and Open Source Software to democratize learning and innovation.

Our initiative provides students with a dynamic, hands-on environment to master cutting-edge technologies while fostering a vibrant culture of collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing.
